WELCOME TO THE WPI
CYCLING CLUB


Road | Mountain | Cyclocross

The WPI cycling club is a club sport with the mission to get people to have a good time on bikes. We do campus cruises to competitive races and everything in between. We usually dedicate A term to mountain biking group rides and races, and give B term some love with road and cyclocross. C term gets a bit chilly so we hold indoor trainer rides. If you love bikes come aboard!
